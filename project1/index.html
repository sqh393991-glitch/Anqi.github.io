Forest Story

Forest Story is an interactive experience controlled by hand gestures using ml5.js handPose. The camera tracks your hands and turns them into characters inside the scene.

The story takes place in a mysterious forest. One hand controls a girl exploring the space, while the other controls a monster following her. When the second hand opens, a tool appears, allowing the girl to defend herself or fight back. Press the space to throw bombs from the girl. Holding the spacebar allows continuous throws, giving the user direct control over the story.

Users actively perform the story rather than just watching it. Movements and gestures directly shape what happens in the scene, making the narrative interactive.
